Online Voting System

The online voting system aims to provide a more convenient and efficient way for citizens to participate in elections. With paper-based voting systems, it can be difficult to locate specific candidates and ensure voter eligibility. It also creates a hectic situation for voters who need to visit the center. The online voting system simplifies this process.
